With over a dozen years of yoga practice, I have come to understand that the purpose of yoga is to facilitate shedding layers of the ego and once again connect to Self.

My lighthearted classes are rooted in joyful and sweaty vinyasa flow. Through working wisely with the body, mind and breath, I encourage students to feel and remember their innate wholeness, experience the well of freedom and love within, and bring that out of the yoga studio and into the world.
